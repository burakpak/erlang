Average call handling time & staffing model for telecom customer service

Input parameters

Call arrival rate 100 calls/hr
Calls reaching your center per hour (λ). Combine talk + abandonment to estimate peak load. Typical call centers: 50–300/hr.
Range: 10 – 500 calls/hr
Average handle time (AHT) 240 s (4:00)
Talk time + hold + after-call work (ACW) per call. Telecom support: typically 3–8 min. Billing queries run longer than tech support triage.
Range: 30 – 900 s
Active agents 12 agents
Simultaneous agents logged in and available. Does not include supervisors, breaks, or training. Erlang C requires this to exceed traffic load.
Range: 1 – 80 agents
Service level target time 20 s
The "T" in "X% of calls answered within T seconds". Industry standard is 20 s for telecom (the 80/20 rule). Some centers use 30 s.
Range: 5 – 120 s
